# Tips for research

1. Read broadly.
2. Work on two things.
3. Be ambitious.
4. Collaborate.
5. Be proactive (in reaching out to others).
6. Write a blog.
7. Keep a source of positive energy.
8. Play to your strengths.
    * Work that is impactful.
    * Work you are passionate about.
    * Work you are uniquely suited for.
9. Intern or visit a university.
10. Play the long game.
